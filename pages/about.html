About Flour & Code

Our story, values, and passion for baking

Our Story

Flour & Code was born from a passion for combining two seemingly different worlds: the art of traditional baking and the precision of modern technology. Founded in 2020, our bakery started as a small neighborhood shop with a big dream.

What began as a weekend hobby blossomed into a beloved community bakery. Every morning, our team arrives before dawn to prepare fresh croissants, artisan breads, and delicate pastries.

Today, Flour & Code stands as a testament to what's possible when passion meets purpose. We serve our community with products that bring joy, comfort, and delicious moments to every table.
